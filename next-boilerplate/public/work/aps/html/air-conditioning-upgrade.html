how it works

  1. 1 Confirm your eligibility
  2. 2 Schedule an appointment with an APS Rebate Eligible Contractor. You must use an APS.
  3. 3 Your contractor will complete these steps and then help you fi ll out and send in the rebate application.
  4. 4 APS processes your application and a rebate check is mailed to you in 6 – 8 weeks
  5. 5 Enjoy peace of mind and comfort with energy savings on your new high effi ciency AC system.

eligibility

  • Must be a current APS customer.
  • Rebate application form must be submitted to APS within 6 months of installation date.
  • Unit must be a new installation that replaces an existing heat pump or air conditioner (AC) in an existing home served by APS.
  • The main components of the unit must be installed within five days of each other.
  • Schedule an appointment with an APS Rebate Eligible Contractor. You must use an APS Rebate Eligible Contractor to qualify for rebates.
